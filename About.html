Let There Be Light...box!
ligthbox is an online drawing tool.
the goal is to help artist learn to draw things (especially those outside his/her comfort zone).

there are other goals that we at artJutsu would like to accomplish.
  • become topical, by sketching views/scene from current affairs and pop culture
  • be part of a community of people who think drawing is fun
  • learn about new art resources and events


how to use?
lightbox is really easy to use. If you have a tablet, or a mouse you are ready to go.
Just draw, learn and have fun.
Everyday a new picture will be shown by default. And you can compare your version with others from the community!
An idea of mine would be to share any link throughout via lightbox. It would be cool to see a link on your social network with a drawing.
